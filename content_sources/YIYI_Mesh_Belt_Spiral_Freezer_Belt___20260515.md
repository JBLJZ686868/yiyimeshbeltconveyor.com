NINGJIN YIYI MESH BELT CO., LTD Spiral Freezer Belt · Why Specification Must Be Right Before Production ▶  A Spiral Freezer Belt Is Not a Standard Conveyor Belt. The belt operates inside a turning, low-temperature, food-processing environment. If the structure is not matched correctly, the system will not run. Drum diameter wrong The belt cannot navigate the drum. Running resistance, jamming, or edge stress appear immediately after installation. The system cannot operate. Spiral pitch incorrect Pitch mismatch against drum geometry causes uneven tension across the belt width. Tracking error and premature link failure follow. Material grade insufficient SUS304 is the standard. SUS316L is required for high-salt or aggressive cleaning environments. A wrong material grade causes corrosion within months. Weld variation batch-to-batch Manual welding produces ±0.5mm variation. One weak joint in a 400m belt is enough to cause failure.
