FAQ

Exhibition & Trade Show FAQ

Which exhibitions does YIYI Mesh Belt attend?+
YIYI Mesh Belt focuses on exhibitions where food-processing, bakery, seafood, meat-processing, and export buyers can evaluate real belt samples in person: IBA Munich, Germany; Shanghai International Bakery Exhibition; Canton Fair, Guangzhou; China Fisheries & Seafood Expo, Qingdao; and China Meat Industry Exhibition. For the current schedule and booth numbers, contact us directly.
Can I arrange a meeting at your IBA booth in advance?+
Yes. We strongly encourage pre-scheduled booth meetings - they allow us to prepare specific belt samples, technical documentation, or a specification review for your project before you arrive. Contact us at least 2 weeks before the show with your product of interest or your belt specification. Our sales director and a technical engineer will be present at the booth throughout the exhibition.
What can I bring to the booth for an immediate specification review?+
The most useful thing is a worn belt sample - even a 30cm section allows us to identify spiral pitch, rod pitch, wire diameter, and edge construction on the spot. If you have the OEM equipment manual or a belt part number from the original supplier, bring that too. A drawing or photo of your conveyor drum is helpful for spiral systems. We carry measurement tools at the booth and can give you a confirmed specification and a sampling commitment before you leave.
Why does YIYI Mesh Belt participate in IBA when most Chinese manufacturers do not?+
We exhibit at IBA because our target customers - European and North American industrial bakers, IQF equipment OEMs, and food processing procurement teams - evaluate suppliers at IBA. A manufacturer who is not present is not in consideration. Maintaining a booth for 5 consecutive years is also a statement of financial stability and market commitment that a single-year appearance is not. We have converted more OEM supply relationships from face-to-face IBA conversations than from any other marketing activity.
I missed the last show. Can I still get the same service?+
Yes - completely. WhatsApp, email, or video call provides the same specification review, sampling discussion, and engineering communication as a booth meeting. Send photos of your belt or your drawing to +86 134 5177 3742 on WhatsApp and we will route it for engineering review. We have initiated and completed full OEM supply agreements remotely - many customers have never visited a show and receive the same specification review and quality process as those who have.
