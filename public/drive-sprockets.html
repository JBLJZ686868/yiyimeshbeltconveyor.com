Why Order Belt & Sprockets Together

One Supplier. Guaranteed Fit. Zero Compatibility Risk.

Belt and sprocket must share the exact same pitch to engage correctly. A mismatch causes accelerated belt wear, tracking problems, and premature failure. When YIYI Mesh Belt supplies both, pitch compatibility is guaranteed by design.

⛓
Exact Pitch Match
Sprocket tooth pitch machined to match belt rod pitch exactly. No approximation. No adjustment required at installation.
△
Extended Belt Life
Correct sprocket geometry distributes drive force evenly across belt width. Prevents edge overloading and premature wire fatigue.
🔄
Simplified Replacement
When your belt wears out, order the replacement with the same YIYI Mesh Belt sprocket reference. No re-measurement, no fitting issues.
🗺
Custom Bore & Keyway
Shaft bore and keyway machined to your shaft specification. Sprocket arrives ready to mount — no on-site machining required.
🏭
All Environments
Stainless for food-contact, high-temp, and corrosive environments. Nylon/POM for lighter-duty, low-noise, and low-contamination applications.
⚙
In-House Machining

How to Order

4-Step Sprocket Ordering Process

Provide belt specification and shaft dimensions. We confirm the design and produce matched sprockets.

01
Send Belt Specification
Provide belt type, wire diameter, rod pitch, and belt width. Or send your existing belt part number — we cross-reference to confirm specification.
02
Confirm Shaft Dimensions
Send shaft diameter, shaft length, keyway dimensions (if required), and number of sprockets per shaft. We machine the bore to fit.
03
Select Material & Grade
Choose stainless (SUS304/316/310S) or nylon (PA6/POM) based on temperature and environment. We confirm the right choice for your application.
04
Production & Delivery
Sprockets machined, pitch verified, bore checked. Shipped with belt as one order or separately as replacement set. Lead time 15–25 working days.
