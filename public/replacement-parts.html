02 - Connecting Accessories

Everything Needed to Join Sections Into One Complete Belt

Long conveyor belts - 50m, 100m, 200m or more - are manufactured and shipped in sections. The connecting accessories included with every YIYI Mesh Belt order allow your installation team to assemble the sections into one continuous belt on-site, without specialist tools or additional procurement.

🕘
Standard inclusion with every order: We calculate the required connecting accessories based on your total belt length and include them with every shipment - extra pins, spare links, cross rods, and belt-type-specific connection hardware. You do not need to order separately.

03 - Drive Sprocket Replacement

Sprockets Matched to Your Belt Pitch and system geometry

Drive sprockets wear with the belt. Replacing a belt with a mismatched sprocket - even marginally - causes uneven drive, premature edge wear, and shortened belt life. YIYI Mesh Belt produces replacement drive sprockets matched to your exact belt specification: tooth profile, pitch circle diameter, shaft bore, and keyway dimensions.

SUS304 / SUS316L stainless steel - full food-grade compliance
Engineering plastic (POM / Nylon) - noise reduction, lighter drive load
Custom shaft bore and keyway - matched to your drive shaft specification
Simultaneous supply - belt and sprockets shipped together to simplify planned maintenance shutdown
FINAL FIT VERIFICATION - belt and sprocket engagement verified together before despatch

FAQ

Replacement & Aftermarket FAQ

I have a worn belt but no drawings or part numbers. Can you still match it?+
Yes - this is the most common situation we handle. Send us photos of the belt from above, from the side, and close-up of the edge construction. If possible, include a measurement of the belt width and a close-up showing the weave pattern. From these photos we can review spiral pitch, rod pitch, wire diameter, and edge type. If you can cut and courier a 30cm section, we can measure everything precisely in our factory before confirming the replacement specification.
What connecting accessories are included with a belt order?+
Every belt order includes a calculated quantity of connecting accessories based on total belt length and belt type. For spiral and self-stacking belts this includes connecting pins, spare links, and cross rods at the section junction points. For eye link belts, connecting tubes are included. The quantity is calculated so your installation team has everything needed to assemble the belt on-site plus a small surplus for field repairs. If you need additional accessories, or if you are repairing an existing YIYI Mesh Belt belt, we can supply accessories separately - contact us with your belt specification and required quantity.
How quickly can you supply in an emergency?+
For customers with an existing specification on file, we can begin production immediately and despatch air freight within 24-48 hours of order confirmation. Air freight delivery is typically 3-7 working days depending on destination and customs clearance. For new customers with an emergency, contact us immediately via WhatsApp with photos or your specification - we will confirm the fastest available timeline within 2 hours. Production lead time for a matched replacement is typically 5-10 working days for common configurations.
Do I need to replace the drive sprockets at the same time as the belt?+
It depends on the condition of your existing sprockets. If the sprockets have significant wear - visible tooth profile deformation, uneven wear marks, or if the belt has been running for more than 2 years - we recommend replacing both simultaneously. Worn sprockets cause accelerated wear on a new belt and reduce belt life substantially. If your sprockets are in good condition, the new belt will run on them without issue. We can supply matched sprockets with any belt order at no additional lead time - they are shipped together.
What is the sample review process?+
For replacement orders where fit is critical, we can prepare a belt sample - typically 0.5m to 1m in length - before committing to full production. The sample is produced to the confirmed specification and sent to you for test-fitting on your system. This confirms drum engagement, edge alignment, and drive performance before we begin the full production run. Sample production usually takes 7-10 working days depending on specification. If the sample does not fit correctly, we adjust the specification before production begins.
