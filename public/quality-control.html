VERIFIED QUALITY SYSTEM

Quality Control Starts Before Production, Not Before Shipment

From incoming raw material verification to final shipment release, YIYI Mesh Belt controls quality across every critical stage of metal conveyor belt manufacturing.

For industrial conveyor belts, quality issues rarely begin at the last step. They usually start earlier - with unstable raw material, uncontrolled tolerance, inconsistent forming, welding variation, or missing verification before shipment. YIYI Mesh Belt's quality control process is built to control these risks before they become delivery problems.

Why Quality Control Starts Before Production

At YIYI Mesh Belt, quality control is not treated as a final sorting step. It begins with traceable material sourcing, incoming inspection, order-based records, pre-production confirmation, process checks, welding control, and final release before packing.

This approach helps reduce dimensional deviation, replacement mismatch, installation issues, and long-term performance risk for OEM and distributor customers.

STEP 04

Standardized Manufacturing Procedures

Each product follows written manufacturing procedures. These procedures are regularly reviewed and updated to reflect current production control requirements.

Standardized documents help maintain process consistency across repeat orders and different product structures - reducing process drift between batches and operators.

STEP 05

In-Process Manufacturing Control

In-process inspection records are maintained during production. Key checkpoints may include pitch, width, rod position, alignment, edge finish, and structural consistency, depending on belt type. The purpose is not only to detect issues but to prevent deviation from accumulating into final delivery problems.

Pitch CheckPitch and dimensional consistency are verified during production.
Width CheckWidth and belt layout are checked before final release.
Rod PositionRod spacing and link alignment are reviewed for stable running.
Edge FinishEdge condition is inspected to reduce installation and running risk.
STEP 06

Welding & Structural Stability Control

Weld quality is not only about whether a joint is complete. It is also about whether structural alignment, stress handling, and welding consistency are controlled during manufacturing.

For conveyor belts, stable running depends on more than weld strength alone - it also depends on structural balance, dimensional alignment, and reduced variation across the full belt assembly.

Automated and controlled welding process reduces joint-to-joint variation
Structural alignment checked after welding to verify dimensional balance
Weld detail inspection confirms completeness and surface condition
Structural consistency across the full belt assembly, not only individual joints

Structural Alignment CheckAlignment is checked across the belt assembly, not only single joints.
STEP 07

100% Final Inspection Before Shipment

Finished conveyor belts go through 100% final inspection before shipment. Shipment release is arranged only after QA pass.

Final inspection may include:

Wire diameter and overall width

OEM VALUE

Why This Matters for OEM Buyers

For OEM builders, distributors, and replacement buyers, quality control is not only about lower defect rates. It is about lower mismatch risk, better installation confidence, stronger repeatability, clearer documentation, and more dependable long-term supply. A more controlled process reduces hidden cost in communication, after-sales handling, and project delay.

Lower Mismatch Risk
Incoming verification, pre-production confirmation, and dimensional control reduce the probability of receiving a belt that does not fit the existing system.
Better Repeatability
Standardized procedures and process records support consistent output across repeat orders without re-confirming every specification from scratch.
Clearer Documentation
Material certificates, inspection records, and traceability documents available to support OEM record requirements and audit-oriented supply chains.
More Dependable Supply
A controlled quality system supports long-term supply confidence - not just one successful order, but consistent delivery across future batches.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is quality control only performed before shipment?

No. At YIYI Mesh Belt, quality control starts from incoming material verification and continues through production, confirmation, and final release.

Can material certificates be provided with the order?

Yes. Material certificates can be supplied when required, and order-based material records can be traced.

Do you support pre-production sample confirmation?

Yes. Sample confirmation and dimensional review are part of risk reduction for OEM and replacement projects.

Do you perform in-process inspection?

Yes. In-process inspection records are maintained during production to control deviation before final delivery.

Is final inspection 100% before shipment?

Yes. Final inspection is completed before shipment release, and shipping is arranged only after QA pass.

Can buyers request third-party audit or external testing?

Yes. YIYI Mesh Belt supports third-party audit and can cooperate with external laboratory testing when required.
