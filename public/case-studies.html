FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions - Replacement & OEM Projects

Answers from our engineering team based on the most common questions from replacement and new installation customers.

Can YIYI Mesh Belt replace a belt from a Western or European OEM supplier?+
Yes. We match replacements for all major Western and European OEM belt specifications. You provide the sample, the OEM's part number, or key dimensions - we identify the full specification and arrange sample confirmation when test-fitting is required before production. Our drum diameter test ensures the replacement runs on your existing system without modification. Many customers in Europe and North America have transitioned from Western-brand belts to YIYI Mesh Belt supply for cost and lead time reasons while maintaining equivalent performance.
How do I send my existing belt information to YIYI Mesh Belt?+
The fastest method is WhatsApp photos - send photos of both faces of the belt, the edge detail, and any visible pitch markings or manufacturer stamps to +86 134 5177 3742. A ruler alongside the belt in the photo helps confirm dimensions. If you have OEM documentation or the belt manufacturer's part number, send that too. We review the specification and follow up with identification or clarifying questions. You do not need all dimensions before contacting us.
What is the typical timeline from first contact to belt delivery?+
Standard replacement timeline: Day 1 - specification confirmed from photos/sample. Days 2-10 - sample prepared when needed. Days 10-14 - customer test-fits sample and confirms. Days 15-25 - full production, drum test, packing, and shipment. Total from first contact to delivery by sea freight: typically 5-6 weeks. For emergency replacements, we prioritise production and can dispatch by air freight - UK and Australian customers have received belts within 22 days of first contact using this route.
What does the sample review process involve?+
We produce a sample to your confirmed specification when test-fitting is required. Typical sample lead time is 7-10 working days depending on specification. The sample should be test-fitted on your system before confirming the production order. We retain a reference sample from your production record for quality benchmark comparison on future orders. If the sample does not fit correctly, we analyse the dimensional gap and adjust the specification before proceeding to production.
What documentation do you provide for EU food processing and export compliance?+
Standard documentation per order: commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, material test certificate (alloy composition and mechanical properties), ISO 9001 certification copy, and drum test validation record. Available on request: EC 1935/2004 application material declaration (EU), third-party inspection report, alloy gun test photo record, HS code advisory for your import country, and MSDS. For OEM customers requiring HACCP compliance documentation, we can provide an electro-polishing certificate and surface roughness measurement report for SUS316L belts.
Can YIYI Mesh Belt supply belts for an ongoing OEM production programme?+
Yes - long-term OEM supply is a core part of our business. Once your belt specification has been confirmed and validated through the sample process, we maintain a production file for your configuration. Repeat orders are produced to the same confirmed benchmark without requiring a new engineering review. Several of our OEM customers have been ordering from the same production file for 5-10 years. We track belt performance at 6 and 12 months for OEM partners - contact us to discuss a supply framework agreement.
