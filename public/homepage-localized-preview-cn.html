How the Homepage Should Handle Factory Proof Media

The video should sit in the first screen as the main proof element. It should show process and capability, not a generic brand montage. Photo slots should appear as supporting evidence blocks in later sections.

  • Video slot: one primary factory capability video near the hero.
  • Photo slot type A: manufacturing evidence such as robotic welding and wire drawing.
