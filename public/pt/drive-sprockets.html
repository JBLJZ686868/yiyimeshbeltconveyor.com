Why Order Belt & Sprockets Together

One Supplier. Verified Fit. Lower Compatibility Risk.

Belt and sprocket must share the exact same pitch to engage correctly. A mismatch causes accelerated belt wear, tracking problems, and premature failure. When YIYI Mesh Belt supplies both, pitch compatibility is checked through drawing review, machining control, and belt-fit confirmation.

⛓
Exact Pitch Match
Sprocket tooth pitch is machined around the belt rod pitch and checked against the required engagement geometry before shipment.
△
Extended Belt Life
Correct sprocket geometry distributes drive force evenly across belt width. Prevents edge overloading and premature wire fatigue.
🔄
Simplified Replacement
When your belt wears out, order the replacement with the same YIYI Mesh Belt sprocket reference. No re-measurement, no fitting issues.
🗺
Custom Bore & Keyway

All Environments

Which Sprocket for Which Belt?

Quick reference for belt type to sprocket material selection.

Belt Type Sprocket Material Temperature Notes
Open-Link Belt SUS304 / Nylon −40 to +350°C SUS316 for seafood / marine environments
Spiral Freezer Belt SUS304 / SUS316 −40 to +350°C Drive and idle drum sprockets matched as set
Rolled Baking Belt SUS304 / Nylon Up to +300°C Biscuit oven: SUS304 standard

Compound Weave Belt SUS304 / SUS316 Up to +900°C SUS316L for food-processing / pharmaceutical
Chain Driven Belt SUS304 / Carbon steel Up to +600°C Chain pitch must match exactly — order with belt
Trapezoidal Mesh Belt SUS304 / Nylon Up to +600°C Turning drum profile matched to belt turning radius
How to Order

4-Step Sprocket Ordering Process

Provide belt specification and shaft dimensions. We confirm the design and produce matched sprockets.

01
Send Belt Specification
Provide belt type, wire diameter, rod pitch, and belt width. Or send your existing belt part number — we cross-reference to confirm specification.
02
Confirm Shaft Dimensions
Send shaft diameter, shaft length, keyway dimensions (if required), and number of sprockets per shaft. We machine the bore to fit.
03
Select Material & Grade
Choose stainless (SUS304/316/310S) or nylon (PA6/POM) based on temperature and environment. We confirm the right choice for your application.
04
Production & Delivery
Sprockets machined, pitch verified, bore checked. Shipped with belt as one order or separately as replacement set. Lead time 15–25 working days.
