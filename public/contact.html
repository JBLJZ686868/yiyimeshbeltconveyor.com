COMMON QUESTIONS

Before You Send
Quick Answers

How quickly will I receive a response?
We guarantee a response within 24 hours on business days (Monday - Friday, 08:00 - 17:00 CST). For urgent inquiries, WhatsApp is the fastest channel - we typically respond within 1-2 hours during business hours.
What information should I include in my inquiry?
The more detail, the faster we can respond accurately. Helpful information includes: belt type, width, pitch, material (SS304/316L), edge treatment, quantity, application (freezer/oven/processing), system manufacturer, and any current belt brand being replaced. Drawings or photos of existing belts are always welcome.
Do you offer samples before mass production?
Yes. Our standard process includes a sample production and approval stage before mass production begins. You receive a physical sample for on-machine testing. Mass production only starts after your written confirmation. We retain a reference sample from every batch.
Can you match or replace belts from other manufacturers?
Yes, this is one of our core services. Send us your existing belt (or detailed photos and measurements), and our engineering team will analyse the specifications and produce a direct replacement. We have experience replacing belts from all major Western and European manufacturers and OEM-specified systems.
What are your minimum order quantities?
MOQ varies by product type and complexity. For many standard belt types, MOQ starts from practical trial quantities rather than rigid high-volume commitments. For custom or OEM projects, MOQ is confirmed case by case based on structure, material, and production setup. Sample sections for qualification and on-machine review are available where needed.
Do you support factory audits and third-party inspections?
Absolutely. We welcome third-party factory audits, quality inspections, and in-person visits. Our facility is ISO 9001 certified and DUNS registered. We can also provide full documentation packages including material test reports, dimensional inspection records, and QC certificates for every shipment.
