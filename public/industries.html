❄️ Sector 01 - IQF & Spiral Freezing

Spiral Freezer Belt.
Built for Continuous Operation at -40°C.

IQF spiral freezer systems demand the highest structural consistency of any belt application. At sub-zero temperatures, belt tension, turn ratio, and weld integrity must remain constant through thousands of operating hours - or the system fails. YIYI Mesh Belt's robotic welding and drum diameter verification process is specifically designed for this requirement.

✓

Temperature range: -40°C to +20°C continuous operation without structural fatigue or weld failure.

✓

Turn ratio precision: Self-stacking collapse ratio engineered to exact drum diameter - verified by drum test before delivery.

✓

Food-grade compliance: SS304 and SS316L material, hygienic edge treatment, full sanitary test report available.

✓

OEM compatibility: Produced to customer drawing. Compatible with all major IQF and spiral freezer OEM systems.

Hygienic, Cleanable,
Corrosion-Resistant.

Meat and seafood processing environments combine high moisture, aggressive cleaning agents, and strict hygiene standards. Belt failure in these lines means product contamination risk and costly downtime. YIYI Mesh Belt's meat and seafood belts are designed for easy cleaning, maximum corrosion resistance, and uninterrupted production flow.

✓

Washdown compatible: Open mesh structure facilitates high-pressure wash-through without product retention or bacteria build-up.

✓

Chemical resistance: SS316L resists chlorine and acid-based CIP cleaning compounds standard in food processing lines.

✓

Sanitary edge treatment: Closed edge options prevent product trapping. Full sanitary test reports available on request.

✓

Replacement ready: We analyse customer's existing belt and produce a direct replacement - same dimensions, same running geometry, faster re-installation.

🍞 Sector 03 - Baking & Ovens

High-Temperature Baking.
Consistent Heat Transfer. Consistent Product.

Baking oven conveyor belts operate at temperatures up to 350°C and must maintain dimensional stability for consistent product browning and throughput. The belt's mesh opening directly affects heat circulation and bake uniformity - which is why bakers choose YIYI Mesh Belt's precision-formed wire structures with tight dimensional tolerances.

✓

Temperature stability: Engineered for 250°C to 350°C continuous oven environments without thermal expansion deformation.

✓

Uniform mesh opening: Consistent spiral pitch and rod spacing ensures even air circulation and reproducible browning across the belt width.

✓

IBA exhibition validated: YIYI Mesh Belt has exhibited baking conveyor solutions at IBA Germany for 5 consecutive years - the world's leading baking industry platform.

✓

Tunnel oven compatible: Available in continuous lengths to 200m+ with matched splicing sections for tunnel oven installations.

High-Speed Snack Lines.
Precision Mesh for Delicate Product.

Snack and biscuit production lines combine high throughput speeds with delicate product handling requirements. YIYI Mesh Belt's biscuit belts and fine-pitch balanced weave structures are engineered specifically for these demands.

✓

Fine product support: Dense mesh opening prevents small snack products from dropping through or deforming under their own weight at high belt speeds.

✓

High-speed stability: Balanced weave structure maintains tracking stability at production line speeds up to 60m/min.

✓

✓

💧 Sector 07 - Can Washing

Can Washing Systems.
Drainage-Optimised Mesh Design.

Beverage and food can washing systems require belts that allow rapid water drainage, resist detergent corrosion, and maintain tight tolerances for can stability at high conveyor speeds. YIYI Mesh Belt's can washing belts are specified for drainage-to-width ratio and structural rigidity to prevent can tipping at production rates.

✓

High drainage ratio: Mesh opening geometry optimised for fast water drainage without can instability or product loss at speed.

✓

Detergent resistant: SS304 resists alkaline detergents and hot water used in industrial can washing tunnels.

✓

Edge retention: Can washing belts available with solid edge rod options to prevent edge wear at guide rail contact points.
