What Causes Conveyor Belt Tracking Problems?

Tracking problems usually come from a mismatch between the belt, the drive, and the running geometry rather than one isolated defect. Replacement mistakes, worn drive components, poor belt squareness, and system misalignment are all common contributors.

Request Replacement ReviewGet Engineering Support
Common Root Causes
  • Geometry mismatch between belt and machine
  • Drive or sprocket wear
  • Replacement supplied without full system review

Why It Matters

  • Tracking issues quickly destroy service life
  • Downtime can come from repeated wrong replacements
  • Engineering review often saves more than repeated trial-and-error ordering

What to Send

  • Photos or video of the running issue
  • Old belt dimensions and drive photos
  • System type and operating conditions

Need Help Diagnosing a Real Tracking Problem?

Send the running evidence and we will route the issue into replacement review or engineering support depending on what the problem suggests.
