Privacy Policy

This page explains how YIYI Mesh Belt collects, uses, retains, and protects information submitted through our website, inquiry forms, and direct communications.

Last updated: May 28, 2026

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to www.yiyimeshbelt.com, operated by YIYI Mesh Belt for industrial conveyor belt sales, OEM projects, distributor support, and replacement review.

For privacy questions, contact us at info@yiyimeshbelt.com or +86 134 5177 3742.

2. What Information We Collect

  • Contact details such as name, company, email, phone number, and WhatsApp number.
  • Technical inquiry details such as belt type, application context, drawings, measurements, and replacement photos.
  • Commercial details relevant to quoting, delivery, and project coordination.

We collect only information needed to answer your inquiry, support an active project, or follow up on a legitimate commercial conversation.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to quote requests and engineering inquiries.
  • To review drawings, confirm belt specifications, and support replacement matching.
  • To prepare samples, quotations, and delivery coordination for active business opportunities.
  • To maintain commercial communication related to a real industrial project.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for advertising or unrelated marketing purposes.

Information may be shared only when required for shipment execution, legal compliance, or direct service delivery tied to your project.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Our site may use basic analytics to understand page usage and traffic patterns. We do not position this site as an ad-tracking property, and we do not build unrelated advertising profiles from your inquiry behavior.

6. Data Retention

We retain inquiry and project data only as long as needed for commercial follow-up, customer support, legal obligations, and operational records related to industrial supply.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information we hold about you, subject to applicable legal and business recordkeeping requirements.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational steps to protect inquiry data and project information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

9. Contact

If you have privacy questions, please contact info@yiyimeshbelt.com or use the contact page to reach our team.
