Spiral Freezer Belt
Self-stacking · Helical path

Dedicated belt for spiral freezing and proofing tower systems. Self-stacking design allows tight helical travel. Available in SUS304/316.

Rolled Baking Belt
Ultra-thin · 1.5mm wire · Tracking tested

Industry standard for biscuit and cracker tunnel ovens. Ultra-thin <3mm profile. One-piece, no mid-belt joints. 100% tracking tested.

Open-Link Belt
Maximum open area · Drainage & airflow

60-70% open area for maximum drainage, airflow, and cooling. Seafood, meat, freezing, washing. SUS316/316L for marine environments.

SeafoodMeatFreezingSUS316L

Balanced Weave Belt
High-temp capable · Versatile

The industry workhorse. Heat treatment furnaces, baking ovens, general conveying. SUS310S available for continuous 1,100°C service.

FurnaceBakingGeneralSUS310S
View Details →
⚙
Compound Weave Belt
Double Z-spiral · Smaller openings

Two spirals per pitch. Higher load capacity and smaller mesh openings than balanced weave. For small products, food processing, and freezing.

Small ProductsFoodFreezing
View Details →
⛓
Chain Driven Belt
Positive drive · No slippage

Side chain positive sprocket drive. Eliminates belt slippage and lateral drift on heavy loads and wide belts. Accurate product positioning.

Heavy LoadWide BeltPositive Drive

Chain Plate Belt
Solid surface · CIP compatible

Solid stainless plates on side chains. No mesh openings. For liquid products, CIP wash, assembly lines, and pharmaceutical applications.

Liquid ProductsCIPAssembly

Lateral flex · Curved conveyors

Trapezoidal spiral for curved, turning, and spiral conveying. Navigates bends standard mesh cannot. Flat product surface maintained through turns.
