How to Match Sprockets and Conveyor Belts

Sprocket matching is not an accessory issue. The sprocket must fit the belt pitch, tooth relationship, and running geometry. When sprockets are guessed or separated from the belt review, tracking and premature wear become much more likely.

See Drive SprocketsSend Your Belt Drawing
Why This Matters
  • Matched supply reduces installation mismatch
  • Replacement jobs often fail because the drive was ignored
  • OEM buyers need one confirmed belt-and-sprocket logic

Key Checks

  • Belt pitch and sprocket tooth relationship
  • Drive profile and bore details
  • Whether the existing system has worn components

Common Mistakes

  • Ordering a new belt without reviewing the existing sprocket
  • Matching only one dimension while ignoring the drive relationship
  • Splitting supply across sources without final system confirmation
