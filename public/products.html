EDGE TREATMENT - 5 STANDARD OPTIONS

Every Edge Treatment.
Made to Your Specification.

Edge finish affects belt tracking, product containment, and drive system compatibility. All five edge treatments available on any belt type.

Edge
Bare Edge
Standard finish. Spiral ends left as-produced. Suitable for most general applications where edge containment is not required.
Edge
Welded Rod Edge
Straight rod welded along each side edge. Reinforces belt edge under lateral load. Standard for high-speed and heavy-duty applications.
Edge
Folded Edge
Spiral ends folded back and welded. Prevents product from snagging on exposed wire ends. Common in food processing lines.
Bar
Solid Bar Edge
Flat solid bar welded to belt edge. Provides rigid guide surface for track-guided conveyor systems. Excellent lateral stability.
Edge
Flanged Edge
L-shaped flange welded to edge. Provides side containment for small products. Prevents lateral drift on incline conveyors.

ACCESSORIES - ALL CUSTOMISABLE

Everything the Belt Needs.
Made at the Same Factory.

All accessories manufactured in-house to the same quality standard as the belt itself. Specified together. Delivered together.

Plate
Side Guard / Side Plate
Vertical plate welded or bolted to belt edge. Prevents product from falling off the belt side. Height and material to specification.
Part
Cross Bar / Transverse Bar
Horizontal bars welded across belt width at set intervals. Supports product on inclined conveyors. Prevents backslide.
Part
Upstanding Spiral / Flights
Spiral wire elements standing vertically on the belt surface. Creates product separation zones. Common in baking and cooling lines.
Part
Connecting Links
Field-replaceable hinge links for belt joining and repair. Matched to belt rod diameter and pitch for seamless integration.
Part
Reinforcement Rod
Additional straight rod inserted through belt spirals at regular intervals. Increases load capacity and reduces belt elongation under tension.
