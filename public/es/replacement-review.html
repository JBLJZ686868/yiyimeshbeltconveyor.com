Choose the Fastest Replacement Entry Point

Replacement projects rarely begin with perfect data. That is normal. The fastest path depends on whether you have only photos, partial dimensions, or a drawing that still needs confirmation against the running system.

  • Best for urgent plant-side replacement requests
  • Use when the original drawing is missing or incomplete
  • Submit Old Belt Photo

I Have Dimensions or a Sample

  • Best for buyers who can provide pitch, width, rod size, or worn belt sections

What to Send

Three Inputs That Improve Replacement Accuracy

Replacement buyers rarely have a perfect drawing. This section reduces friction while still improving the match.

Visual Proof

  • Overall belt photo
  • Close-up of mesh structure
  • Edge treatment photo

Measured Details

  • Width and usable length
  • Pitch or rod spacing if known
  • Material grade if documented

Running Context

  • Machine type
  • Temperature and cleaning conditions
  • How urgently the line needs the replacement
