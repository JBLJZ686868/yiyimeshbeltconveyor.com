FAQ

Questions Buyers Usually Ask
Before Sending a Drawing

Quick answers for OEM teams, distributors, and replacement buyers who need to understand how to start cooperation with YIYI Mesh Belt.

01

Can YIYI review drawings?

Yes. Send Your Belt Drawings, photos, dimensions, material requirements, and application conditions for technical review.

02

Can you support replacement belts?

Yes. Old belt photos, pitch, rod size, width, and sprocket details help reduce matching risk.

03

Do you supply matched sprockets?

Yes. Belt and sprocket matching can be reviewed together when drive engagement is critical.

04

What proof can buyers check?

Factory capability, robotic welding, in-house wire drawing, QA process, certificates, and export records can be reviewed.

05

Which products are most important?

Spiral Freezer Belt, Self-Stacker Belt, and Eye Link Belt are the current priority tank pages.

06

How should I start?

If you have data, send a drawing. If you are still comparing, Ask For A Quick Quote or talk to an engineer first.
