Buyer Paths

Three Buyer Types, Three Conversion Routes

The homepage should not only say who YIYI is. It should move each buyer type toward the next best action as quickly as possible.

OEM Equipment Builders

  • Home to core product page
  • Then to Engineering & OEM
  • Then to drawing submission or technical discussion

Distributors and Importers

  • Home to Products or Resources
  • Then to Global Delivery
  • Then to catalog, market support, and commercial inquiry

Replacement Buyers

  • Home or product page to Replacement Review
  • Submit old belt photos or dimensions
  • Move into reverse matching and urgent inquiry support
Homepage Structure

Six Homepage Blocks Worth Keeping

This structure fits YIYI's current positioning best because it supports SEO, paid traffic, and industrial conversion at the same time.

1. Positioning Hero

One defining statement, two primary CTAs, and a numeric trust strip.

2. Core System Products

Lead with Spiral Freezer Belt and Self-Stacker Belt before the broader range.

3. Manufacturing Proof

Move manufacturing assurance, QA, and raw-material control into the homepage trust layer.

4. Industry Relevance

Show IQF, baking, food processing, heat treatment, and industrial conveying use cases.

5. Buyer Entry Module

Present distinct actions for OEM, distributor, and replacement intent.

6. Knowledge and Resources

Send search-driven and AI-driven visitors into the Knowledge Center and Resources hub.
